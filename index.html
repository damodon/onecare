Step 2 of 5 — Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

How we handle your health data.

Data We Collect

Account information (name, email), glucose readings from CGM devices or manual entry, meal and nutrition logs, activity data, insulin doses, mood entries, sleep data from connected services, device information for push notifications.

How We Use Your Data

To display your glucose trends and patterns, generate AI-powered summaries for healthcare providers, send alerts when glucose exceeds your thresholds, provide insights on meal and activity impact on glucose levels.

Data Storage

All health data is stored encrypted (AES-256) in AWS data centres located in Australia (ap-southeast-2). Data is transmitted over TLS 1.3. Encryption keys are stored in AWS KMS.

Your Rights

Under the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and applicable APP guidelines you have the right to access, correct, and request deletion of your personal information. Contact privacy@glucolens.com.au to exercise these rights.

Data Sharing

We never sell your data. We share data only: with service providers under strict data processing agreements, with family/carer contacts you explicitly invite, when generating AI summaries (anonymised for API processing).

Retention

We retain your data while your account is active and for 12 months following account deletion, after which it is permanently purged.

Privacy Officer: privacy@glucolens.com.au
